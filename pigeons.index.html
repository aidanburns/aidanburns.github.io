PIGEONS

it was dark out
the town was dimly lit
and there was a noise coming from somewhere

the people buzzed like flies
they moved quickly, anxiously
together, away from whatever was coming for them

they had heard it before, but nothing like now
they decreed it true,
the Light was coming, the Wall of Fire!
for them!
the Supernova!

they started to move,
away, through dark alleys and the tunnels
but where are they going?
not a global migration but a collective knotting into,
as they grew weary and short of breath,
they slowed and shielded their eyes

and all that was left
were their bags
and the pigeons
