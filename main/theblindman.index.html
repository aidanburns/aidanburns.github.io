THE BLIND MAN

it crawled in slowly, then all at once
like black tar, thick dust around my eyes and mouth
reaching inside and coating my throat
constricting my fingers, i hold on
tighter, longer, self-emulation

yet you break through and it’s disappeared, broken
through like a dagger, righteous, sunlight -
then flooding back, away from me,
it never happened, you were never there, still

all is the same, all
covered in your ink, it can’t be clean,
it can’t be moved, touched, your
arms pushed, contorted
to your sides, as it should be, cleanly
