ELECTROMAGNETIC GRENADE

i was a tumor until the world cut me away
we fed off each other
peace was established then broken again
i contained your anger and maintained your image

i sent a message to you
you were too blind to see
what we were doing to each other
you stood on me and talked about your problems

i grew away
i grew out of you

we developed in isolation there
we changed over time
i still believed you, i hadn't learned

i watched you move away slowly
in the back of my head
i look across the room and see just how far you've fallen
