AUGUST

it’s a last hurrah for everything you loved
everything’s the best it was going to be
it’s a drawn-out encore
it’s the best part,
but with that,
you get the creeping feeling that

nothing’s like it should be
there’s a collaborative tension,
people speak with an air of unease and finality
everyone’s running out, running dry
in air that’s still a little too warm
to be comfortable

maybe you can forget,
maybe it doesn’t affect you
but you can still see it, right?
the weight looming overhead,
everything you thought you left behind,
coming back,
by its nature-
you didn’t think it’d be gone forever, did you?
