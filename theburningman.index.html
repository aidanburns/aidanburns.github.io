24/7/2019

THE BURNING MAN

coughing and spluttering
his blood spills onto the cobblestones
the onlookers hold out their hands in appraisal

he is drowned out by their adoration
it flows through his open ribcage and into his heart
it contorts his legs and arms

he rises on his wooden table,
a shell.
he raises his arms to the sky
the sun glows through his veins,

his skin like paper,
he collapses into the air, weighing nothing and
made whole by their thunderous applause
