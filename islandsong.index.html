ISLAND SONG

she dreampt of a world full of dogs
she sat in a field and whistled her song
they came running over the crumpling walls

she fed them with a spoon made of oak
in small wooden bowls, the dogs took them
with them back into the forest

her own stayed by her side, chewed on the
spoon and sat in her lap, it seemed to melt into the grass

they sat like ancient marble statues life that for
years. she left one day, into the sky, slowly. she
looked down to her island, it was the most
beautiful thing she'd ever seen.
