PARADE

the walls had stood there for as long as they could remember
so many attempts to tear them down had left them
scratched and cracked everywhere

after so long, they found him
the one to free them
he walked through the city to cheering crowds

they gathered round as he smiled,
slipping into the crack in the wall,
disappearing into the blackness

slowly, the walls fell that day,
first dust,
then everything came tumbling down,
spilling over itself, violently
smashing and shattering as it went

long after they left the city behind,
when they took their children by the hand
and walked them over the rubble,
his name was long forgotten,
but what he did would stand forever
