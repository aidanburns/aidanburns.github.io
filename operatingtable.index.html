OPERATING TABLE

she said,
‘gather round children’

she took the scalpel and cut,
displaying the parts on a tray to the side

a few smiled, they were interested
‘now that’s how they did it’

she brought out another tray
full with little wooden scalpels

30 scalpels for 30 children
and after being taught the right technique

they started digging in themselves
pulling out sections and putting them back together again

she pulled up a diagram
and showed them what everything did

that day they picked it clean
scraping all they could from every word

as they stared at the pile
their smiles turned to shrugs

the magic was gone
it was all laid bare in front of them

she started to build,
a tower, laced with meaning and extravagance

the kids tried to follow, but they couldn’t
how could they write about something
that wasn’t there anymore?
