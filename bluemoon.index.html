BLUE MOON

it happens about the same time every year
thousands come, shambling over the mountains

they could see through the fog
something had gotten inside of them
their guts were their compasses, weighing them down

down from over the hills they came
through the husks of burnt out buildings

one at a time, they would lay down
their arms outstretched
drive nails through their hands and look up at the burning tree

collapsing inwards,
they gave themselves to the blaze
