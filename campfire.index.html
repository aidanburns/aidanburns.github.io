CAMPFIRE

they sit, open eyed
around the hearth

their skeletons are on the fire
the only sound for miles around
is them cracking and falling apart
their skin left behind ninety minutes ago
it’s like it’s something new

you take it out piece by piece
and one by one
you break them over your knee
and throw them into the blaze

it’s someone new
that took the bucket
and dowsed the flames
