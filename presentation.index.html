PRESENTATION

if you can put fire in a jar
you can bottle anything
strip it down to its core elements,
sparkling water
chewy lemon and lime sweets
coming home after a scorching day
you can bundle them into a cart
tie red string with bows
decorate labels and press them on neatly

untie your horse
and set off down the dusty path at sunrise
reaching the market early,
you unload your wares,
let your horse drink from the fountain
and sell it all back to the people
