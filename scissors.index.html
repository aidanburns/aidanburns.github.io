SCISSORS

there’s a man at the back of the theatre
pulling on a line of string
drawing it together, close to his chest

it’s looped through the back of your neck,
running back to your friends, to tangles

it’s best seen in contrast
when one breaks free of the knots
and is dragged backwards,
grasping at nets of red yarn

the bus stops, and you see it,
there’s the tug,
loops slowly coming undone,
everything unravelling in a spiral pattern
