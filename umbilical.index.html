UMBILICAL

it’s the start of something new
all he’s known is a tiny part of what he sees

there are other things out there
colour and sound and warmth
and gunpowder and storms and
things he couldn’t have imagined
with such a small sense of the world
and everything else

somewhere above, there was music playing
and he floated,
falling in place forever
he took his last look at the frontier
and reeled himself back in
