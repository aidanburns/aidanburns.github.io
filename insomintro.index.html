the insomniacs is a text-based adventure game about exploring a strange world.
here are a few things you should know before you begin.

the game uses your browser cookies to constantly save your progress.
when you launch the game, it should pick up where you left off.

you may wish to take notes or pictures while playing.

for the best experience, i recommend playing in longer sessions on a computer instead of with a phone or tablet.
please take your time and explore thoroughly. i hope you enjoy.
