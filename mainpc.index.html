tell me that I’m a phantom
and that I’m not really here
I float through the halls and the voices echo
inside around for hours
he sees himself with a third person camera
he wants to change his name
but leave halfway through the procedure
he wants to leave himself an incomplete person in the eyes of the law

are you aware of your single remaining life?
keep searching for another one, go through
your friends desks and turn the objects over,
keep writing the names and places flow from
your head again

he needs to know something else is there
he needs to converse with us at last
you need to bury his feet in the dirt
and have him talk for as long as he can
don't let him get up
