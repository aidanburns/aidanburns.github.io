FACE THE LIGHTS

take all that you know
and put it before a board of judges
they’re standing there, strong in the invisible crowd

they all begin to chant, force your hand,
the four are marching, hammers in tow
swinging, they bring them down, at once
there was nothing they could hide behind

take to the floor and put it all back together
join wood and metal, drive it all together
next time they’ll be ready
