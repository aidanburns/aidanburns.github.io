THE CYBORG

micheal drives a blue ford focus,
eyes blinking in the backseat,
the lights form shadow puppets on the dashboard,
moving slowly back and forth,
the world flattens into paper
and lays itself on the ground,
his blue dot glides across the rocks,
but quickly ahead is a glass storefront,
he sees himself, his pupils dilate for one moment,

cries of metal, husks, armour,
the others step away,
he was lifted above, an ambassador,
the light of one thousand tiny suns shining through him
