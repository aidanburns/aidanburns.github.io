A LOST RADIO TOWER OFF THE COAST OF ALASKA

their little boat
emerged from the cave
slowly, rocked side to side
and set a course for home

the crew,
in orange hi-vis jackets
held onto the sides,
sat and whistled
then their sensors picked up something

a large metal structure
two thousand metres below the surface and sinking,
slowly, they retrieved their hooks
and threw them over the side,
the rope whirring as it reached farther and farther down
until

they hooked the mesh
and brought it back up

it was a few minutes
until they saw what they found

a blinking red light,
fixed to a radio tower,
wires and all

a few reached for their radios
a few secured the tower to their tiny boat
and one looked up and saw the sun
rising over the ocean
at once, their jackets shone a great yellow
they heard an acoustic guitar coming from the radio
the waves drowned out the static

they stood,
in awe of it all for a little while

and the engine kicked in
and they sped off
back to record their findings
