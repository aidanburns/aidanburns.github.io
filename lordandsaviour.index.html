LORD AND SAVIOUR

Flying is nothing to be feared anymore.
A dull whine permeates the morning sky,
it shakes the lights in their fittings,
shadows play across the grey speckled floors.

Are we waiting for the Americans? People
who know what they’re doing with these types of things.
Planes have crashed together in the sky,
the nation is in mourning.

They wish to construct a great tower to show
our strength, cower around its cast iron beams and
pray that whatever mechanical bird or
foe will come home to rest.

We are not the ones here, it is them,
If and when we leave, there will be others in our place.
Don’t be concerned with the small things. Look up.
